Behind Elevate Maths

Meet Your Tutor.

My name is Sajan. I created Elevate Maths because I believe the traditional approach to GCSE maths limits what students can achieve. Most people see it as a daunting, exhausting subject — but a top grade isn't mysterious. It comes down to three things: a structured plan, consistent practice and the right guidance.

I'll handle the full journey with your child from start to finish. We break every topic into small, manageable chapters and build through repetition — the method that actually works. I've proven this works on myself. Over the last three years, I passed 13 ACCA exams through self-study using these exact techniques. That's not theory. That's a system I've refined and implemented throughout Elevate.

Your child won't just learn the content. They'll develop the mindset and discipline needed to solve hard problems independently — something that matters far beyond maths.

The Process

Five Steps to Greatness.

01

Book a Call

Submit an enquiry and we'll arrange a call to discuss your child's goals, current level, and which cohort is right for them.

02

Discovery Session

Your child completes a free diagnostic session. We assess capability, learning style, and group placement.

03

Enrol & Begin

Sign the engagement letter, complete payment, and receive your child's starter pack. Sessions begin on the cohort start date.

04

Track Progress

Termly reports, regular homework accountability, and open study sessions ensure your child stays on track.

05

Results

Celebrate your child's successes and look forward to the new opportunities that their hard work and dedication have unlocked.
